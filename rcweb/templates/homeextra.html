Its always our moral duty to give something back to the community when we have recieved everything from it. Giving this platform a social aspect.
"No one can help everyone, but everyone can help someone."
And Reverse Classroom wishes to take a step forward, and do something to help. Hence we plan on arranging classes for underprivileged children on various topics, by collaborating with various NGO's and other social platforms.






Our Vision

We at Reverse Classroom, keeping in mind the growing and changing needs of society and industry, are solemnly committed to create an environment which will raise the intellectual and cultural standards of all students and peers. Our endeavour is to strive for the holistic development of students by reversing the standard student-teacher relationship therefore transforming a passive learner into an active teacher or learner. In tune with this our vision is :
▪️To build sustainable classrooms for various technical and creative skills, since knowledge shared = Knowledge2
▪️Sharing knowledge free of cost & expanding this platform to various colleges across the country.
▪️Providing opportunities for the exchange of knowledge and ideas between like minded people.

Why reverse classroom:

Hasn't it happened often, that you don't understand a single thing in class, and then have a friend explain it all to you later? It just feels so comfortable and easy sharing and exchanging information amongst our peers right?
How is it different from other sources of learning
An online seminar, a recorded course or a normal class. All share the same aim to share knowledge. Today there are so many courses available online, so many resources to study from, and yet the touch and feel of the classroom is something which cannot be changed. You sit with you friends, chat a little, discuss, have fun, and learn along they way, instead of just staring at the screen to learn all day. We aim to exchange knowledge because sharing is one-way street --> exchanging is a two-way street We at reverse classroom bring that kind of a conducive environment into our classes. The teachers or professors helping you out will be students just like you, and more accomplished in the particular field they teach. We plan on hosting smaller classrooms with a maximum of 15 learners which would provide individual attention to each and everyone; related to various subjects, academic as well as non academic. If you have certain skills you would like to share and teach, this could be your platform for that too. You'll get more seva/satva hours for doing so too. Needless to say, the classes will be free and open to anyone with the enthusiasm to learn new things. Moreover being taught by your peer would give an added confidence to the learner as well as the teacher thus bettering the learning process! Hence more involving and communicative sessions.
